The Great Wave off Kanagawa

Artist: Katsushika Hokusai

Year: 1831

Medium: Woodblock print

About: A towering wave looms over fishing boats, with Mount Fuji calmly framed in the distance.

Fibonacci Insight: The wave’s crest follows a near-perfect spiral, sweeping the viewer’s eye through controlled turbulence.
